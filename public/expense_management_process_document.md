# Digital Expense Management System Implementation - Current Process Document

## Project Overview
**Company:** FinanceFirst Corp  
**Project:** Digital Expense Management System Implementation  
**Current Timeline:** 2-3 weeks for expense reimbursement  
**Target Timeline:** 3-5 business days  
**Current Error Rate:** 12%  
**Target Error Rate:** <1%

## Current 10-Step Expense Process

### Step 1: Employee Expense Submission
- **Action:** Employee completes paper expense form and attaches physical receipts
- **Who:** Employee, Direct Manager
- **Duration:** 1-2 days
- **Real Challenges:**
  - Employees lose receipts or forget to submit on time
  - Paper forms get damaged or lost in transit
  - No backup if original receipts are misplaced
  - Employees have to manually calculate totals
  - No real-time validation of expense categories
  - Forms often incomplete or illegible

### Step 2: Manager Initial Approval
- **Action:** Form submitted to direct manager for initial approval
- **Who:** Direct Manager, Employee
- **Duration:** 2-3 days
- **Real Challenges:**
  - Managers often traveling or unavailable for signatures
  - No policy validation at this stage
  - Managers don't have access to budget information
  - Approval delays cause employee frustration
  - No audit trail of approval decisions
  - Managers can't easily check policy compliance

### Step 3: Manual Form Processing
- **Action:** Manager reviews and signs form manually
- **Who:** Direct Manager, Finance Operations
- **Duration:** 1 day
- **Real Challenges:**
  - Manual signature collection takes time
  - No digital record of approval
  - Forms can be lost between manager and finance
  - No notification when forms are received
  - Managers can't approve remotely
  - No version control if forms are revised

### Step 4: Finance Operations Handoff
- **Action:** Form forwarded to Finance Operations via internal mail
- **Who:** Finance Operations Team, Mail Room
- **Duration:** 1-2 days
- **Real Challenges:**
  - Internal mail delays during peak periods
  - Forms can be lost in transit
  - No tracking of form location
  - Finance team doesn't know when to expect forms
  - No prioritization of urgent expenses
  - Mail room processing adds unnecessary delay

### Step 5: Manual Data Entry
- **Action:** Finance team manually enters data into Excel spreadsheet
- **Who:** Finance Operations Specialist, Accounting Team
- **Duration:** 2-3 days
- **Real Challenges:**
  - High error rate from manual data entry
  - Duplicate entries common
  - No validation during entry
  - Excel formulas can be accidentally changed
  - No backup of original data
  - Time-consuming and repetitive work

### Step 6: Policy Compliance Review
- **Action:** Accounting reviews for policy compliance and mathematical accuracy
- **Who:** Accounting Specialist, Finance Manager
- **Duration:** 1-2 days
- **Real Challenges:**
  - Manual policy checking is time-consuming
  - Inconsistent application of policies
  - No automated flagging of violations
  - Mathematical errors often missed
  - No integration with HR policy system
  - Review quality depends on individual attention

### Step 7: Exception Handling
- **Action:** Exceptions flagged and returned to employee via email
- **Who:** Accounting Specialist, Employee, Manager
- **Duration:** 3-5 days
- **Real Challenges:**
  - Email communication is slow and unreliable
  - Employee may not see email for days
  - No tracking of exception resolution
  - Multiple back-and-forth emails required
  - No escalation process for unresolved issues
  - Employee frustration with unclear requirements

### Step 8: Payment Processing
- **Action:** Approved expenses entered into NetSuite for payment processing
- **Who:** Accounting Specialist, NetSuite System
- **Duration:** 1-2 days
- **Real Challenges:**
  - Manual entry into NetSuite creates delays
  - No integration between systems
  - Payment processing errors common
  - No real-time status updates
  - Employee can't track payment status
  - Bank reconciliation issues

### Step 9: Physical Check Distribution
- **Action:** Physical check printed and mailed to employee
- **Who:** Finance Operations, Mail Room, Employee
- **Duration:** 3-5 days
- **Real Challenges:**
  - Physical checks can be lost in mail
  - Employee may be traveling when check arrives
  - No tracking of check delivery
  - Checks can be stolen or misplaced
  - Employee has to physically deposit check
  - No electronic payment option

### Step 10: Monthly Reconciliation
- **Action:** Monthly reconciliation performed manually against bank statements
- **Who:** Finance Manager, Accounting Team
- **Duration:** 2-3 days monthly
- **Real Challenges:**
  - Manual reconciliation is error-prone
  - Discrepancies often discovered late
  - No real-time visibility into expenses
  - Difficult to identify patterns or issues
  - No automated reporting
  - Time-consuming monthly process
