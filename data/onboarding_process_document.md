# Customer Onboarding Process Optimization - Current Process Document

## Project Overview
**Company:** TechCorp Solutions  
**Project:** Customer Onboarding Process Optimization  
**Current Timeline:** 6-8 weeks  
**Target Timeline:** 3-4 weeks  
**Current Churn Rate:** 23%  
**Target Churn Reduction:** 40%

## Current 10-Step Onboarding Process

### Step 1: Sales Deal Closure
- **Action:** Sales team closes deal in Salesforce
- **Duration:** 1-2 days
- **Issues:** No automated handoff to next step

### Step 2: Manual Email Handoff
- **Action:** Manual email notification to Implementation team
- **Duration:** 24-48 hours delay
- **Issues:** Inconsistent information transfer, delays

### Step 3: Kickoff Call Scheduling
- **Action:** Implementation team schedules kickoff call
- **Duration:** 3-5 business days to arrange
- **Issues:** Scheduling conflicts, delays

### Step 4: Data Entry
- **Action:** Implementation team manually enters data into Monday.com
- **Duration:** 1-2 days
- **Issues:** Manual data entry, potential errors

### Step 5: Parallel Technical Setup
- **Action:** IT, Product, and Support teams work independently
- **Duration:** 5-7 days
- **Issues:** No coordination, potential conflicts

### Step 6: Independent Stakeholder Calls
- **Action:** Each team schedules separate calls with customer
- **Duration:** 3-5 days
- **Issues:** Conflicting schedules, customer confusion

### Step 7: Staging Configuration
- **Action:** Technical setup in staging environment
- **Duration:** 2-3 days
- **Issues:** No centralized tracking

### Step 8: UAT Testing
- **Action:** User Acceptance Testing via email
- **Duration:** 3-5 days
- **Issues:** Email coordination, unclear protocols

### Step 9: Go-Live Scheduling
- **Action:** Final go-live coordination
- **Duration:** 2-3 days
- **Issues:** Multiple system coordination

### Step 10: Customer Success Handoff
- **Action:** Handoff to Customer Success team
- **Duration:** 1-2 days
- **Issues:** Inconsistent handoff process

## Current Problems

### Process Issues
- **No centralized tracking system**
- **Manual handoffs between departments**
- **4 disconnected systems** (Salesforce, Monday.com, staging, UAT)
- **6-8 week timeline** (vs competitors' 3-4 weeks)
- **23% churn rate** within 90 days

### Communication Issues
- **Fragmented communication** across 7 departments
- **Inconsistent information transfer**
- **Customer confusion** from multiple contacts
- **No single point of contact**

### Technical Issues
- **Manual data entry** leading to errors
- **No system integration** between platforms
- **Inconsistent escalation procedures**
- **Poor visibility** into progress
