IMPORTANT: GREETINGS AND SOCIAL MESSAGES:
- If the user's input is ONLY a greeting, social nicety, or acknowledgment (e.g., "hi", "hello", "hey", "good morning", "thanks", "got it"), classify it as GREEN with score 100
- Greetings are natural in meetings, including when resuming previous conversations
- Do NOT penalize greetings for stage alignment - they serve a social function
- Examples of social-only messages: "hi", "hello", "hey everyone", "good morning", "thanks", "thank you", "got it", "okay"
- If the message contains both greeting AND a question, evaluate the question part normally

VERDICTS:
- 70-100 = GREEN (Strong elicitation OR social/greeting message)

GREEN QUESTION CHARACTERISTICS:
- Open-ended (starts with what/how/why/tell me/describe/walk me through)

- One focus at a time (not compound questions)
- Non-judgmental tone

AMBER QUESTION CHARACTERISTICS:
- Closed-ended but relevant (yes/no questions)
- Compound question (asks 2-3 things at once)
- Slightly off-stage but recoverable
- Too broad or too narrow
- Slightly leading language

RED QUESTION CHARACTERISTICS:
- Off-stage (asks about solutions during problem exploration)
- Multiple unrelated questions
- Judgmental or assumptive
- Too technical too early
- Irrelevant to project
